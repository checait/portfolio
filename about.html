About Me

Hi! My name is Caitlin, and I'm currently pursuing a design degree at UC Davis. When I'm not in Davis, I'm likely back home in New Jersey.

I've loved art since I was little, and I find that creativity seeps into my different parts of my life as well, like hobbies. I was only introduced to UI/UX around my second year of college. The concept of user-centered design really resonated with me, and I liked how collaborative and back-and-forth the process was. It's also fun to keep up with ever-changing design trends.
